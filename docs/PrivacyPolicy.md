## 信息的访问与使用

Select Unknown 不会收集、存储或上传您的个人信息至开发者服务器。

在您主动触发功能时（例如按下快捷键），软件可能会访问以下内容：

- 您当前选中的文本
- 剪贴板内容（仅用于辅助获取选中文本）
- 当前屏幕内容（仅在您主动触发截图功能或 “框索未知” 功能时）

选中文本仅在您主动操作时读取，并用于执行您请求的功能（例如搜索或翻译）。软件不会将该文本上传至开发者控制的服务器。

如果您选择通过内置 WebView 使用在线搜索、翻译或图像识别服务，相关文本或图像内容可能会被发送至您访问的第三方服务提供方。这些数据处理行为由对应服务提供方负责，而非本软件开发者。

屏幕截图仅在您主动触发时生成，不会在后台自动采集。

## 本地数据存储
软件可能在您的设备本地保存以下数据：
- 操作历史记录
- 内置 WebView 在本地生成的浏览数据（例如缓存、Cookie、会话数据等）
- 用户截图文件
- 用户配置文件
- 运行过程中产生的日志（临时文件）

上述数据仅存储在您的本地设备中，不会自动上传，除非您主动通过第三方服务进行数据提交或上传。

默认情况下：

- 数据存储于程序目录
- 临时文件存储于 AppData\Local\Temp 目录

### English Version

## Access and Use of Information

Select Unknown does not collect, store, or upload your personal information to the developer's servers.

When you actively trigger a feature (e.g., by pressing a shortcut key), the software may access the following:

- **Currently selected text**
- **Clipboard content** (used only as an auxiliary method to retrieve selected text)
- **Current screen content** (only when you actively trigger the screenshot or "Select Unknown" features)

Selected text is read only during your active operations and is used solely to perform the functions you request (such as searching or translating). The software does not upload this text to servers controlled by the developer.

If you choose to use online search, translation, or image recognition services through the built-in WebView, the relevant text or image content may be sent to the third-party service providers you access. Such data processing is the responsibility of the respective service providers, not the developer of this software.

Screenshots are only generated when you actively trigger them and are not captured automatically in the background.

## Local Data Storage

The software may save the following data locally on your device:
- Operation history
- Browsing data generated locally by the built-in WebView (e.g., cache, cookies, session data, etc.)
- User screenshot files
- User configuration files
- Logs generated during operation (temporary files)

The above data is stored only on your local device and will not be uploaded automatically, unless you actively submit or upload data through a third-party service.

By default:
- Data is stored in the **program directory**.
- Temporary files are stored in the `AppData\Local\Temp` directory.
